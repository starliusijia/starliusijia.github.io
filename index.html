Proposal Updated

The beginning image of my work will be like this:

When you click one of the titles, the system will enter to the visualized poem animation.

About the visual part, I have two plans.
The first plan is, creating abstracted animation that based on the poem. Each time when you click the title button, you will see the different one.
I may create four particle generators based on the thesis of the poem. The effect are supposed to be as similar as follow:
https://wangyasai.github.io/Particles-Emission/

Another plan is, creating something that figurative, and not so abstract, which can help audience to understand the poem better. I may make a series of scene that describe a piece of poem.

Both of the plan will be use Processing or P5.js as tool.
